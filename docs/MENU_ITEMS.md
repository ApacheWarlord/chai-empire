# Menu Items and Flavor

## Core Chai
- Basic Chai, simple everyday tea, fast to serve, trusted by everyone.
- Masala Chai, warm spice blend, richer aroma, crowd favorite.
- Adrak Chai, fresh ginger kick, especially loved in rain and winter.
- Elaichi Chai, softer cardamom note, feels a little premium.
- Kulhad Chai, earthy clay-cup serving, higher satisfaction and style.

## Snacks
- Rusk Pack, low-cost add-on, common with morning chai.
- Biscuit Pack, quick impulse buy, good starter combo.
- Bun Maska, buttery and comforting, strong tea-shop feel.
- Samosa Plate, filling and high demand during rush hours.
- Bread Omelette, hearty snack, popular with students and workers.

## Extra Unlocks
- Filter Coffee, useful in later shops, broadens the audience.
- Lemon Tea, lighter option, good for variety.
- Cold Drink Bottle, easy add-on for hot afternoons.

## Tone Notes
- Familiar first, premium later.
- Flavor text should feel warm, appetizing, and easy to understand.
- Keep item names short for UI, use flavor text for personality.
