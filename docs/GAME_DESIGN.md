# Chai Empire - Game Design Concept

## Game Type
Offline idle-upgrade business sim for mobile.

## Session Fantasy
The player feels like a smart Indian entrepreneur scaling a chai business from a tiny stall into a local empire.

## Primary Loop
- customers auto-spawn
- available workers auto-serve them
- player earns money continuously
- player buys upgrades to increase output and value
- player eventually upgrades venue tier
- new venue introduces more capacity and item depth

## Core Systems

### 1. Customer Spawn System
Variables:
- base arrival rate
- time-of-day multiplier
- venue popularity
- active event modifiers
- queue capacity

Customer types:
- office worker
- student
- rickshaw driver
- traveler
- local uncle group
- family group
- premium customer

Each type differs in:
- patience
- order value
- item preferences
- likelihood of repeat visits

### 2. Order System
In v1, sales can be semi-automatic:
- customer arrives
- game picks from unlocked items based on demand weights
- worker prepares item automatically
- customer pays and leaves

Later depth:
- combos
- larger groups
- special event orders
- premium bulk orders

### 3. Item Unlock Progression
Tier 1:
- basic chai

Tier 2:
- masala chai
- biscuit pack

Tier 3:
- ginger chai
- bun maska

Tier 4:
- samosa
- coffee

Tier 5:
- bread omelette
- kulhad chai

Item stats:
- prep time
- sale price
- popularity
- satisfaction modifier

### 4. Upgrade Tree
#### Operations
- kettle capacity
- brewing speed
- serving speed
- counter efficiency

#### Staff
- helper
- server
- cashier
- cleaner
- shift supervisor

#### Quality
- tea leaves
- milk quality
- spice blend
- cup quality

#### Marketing
- hand-painted board
- local pamphlets
- word-of-mouth bonus
- social buzz later

#### Customer Comfort
- stools
- shade/tent
- fan
- covered waiting area

### 5. Venue Evolution
Each venue has caps on:
- worker slots
- item slots
- customer queue size
- maximum popularity
- decoration level

Upgrade path:
- Stall -> Kiosk -> Small Shop -> Busy Shop -> Cafe -> Premium Lounge -> Chain Outlet

Venue change gives strong psychological reward:
- visual transformation
- new sounds/theme
- new customer classes
- higher earning ceiling

### 6. Satisfaction and Retention
Satisfaction depends on:
- wait time
- item quality
- item availability
- crowding

High satisfaction creates:
- tips
- repeat customers
- positive buzz multiplier

Low satisfaction creates:
- customers leaving queue
- lower reputation growth
- slower demand scaling

### 7. Endless Scaling
For the endless feel:
- no final ending
- costs and earnings keep scaling
- unlock city zones later
- optional prestige system after several venues

Possible prestige concept:
- reset business but keep "legendary chai recipe" bonuses
- permanent boosts to demand, quality, and premium customers

## Difficulty Philosophy
- relaxing, not punishing
- early game should feel fast and rewarding
- middle game should emphasize smart upgrade sequencing
- late game should feel abundant and aspirational

## Visual Direction
- bright Indian street color palette
- readable top-down or side-view management layout
- iconic props: kettle, cups, stove, hanging biscuits, steel containers, chalkboard menu

## Audio Direction
- kettle boil loop
- cup clink
- light bazaar ambience
- cash sound
- rain ambience during weather events

## Unique Indian Hooks
- weather-driven tea demand spikes
- cricket match viewing crowds
- winter bonus demand
- festival décor and footfall boosts
- location flavor in later expansion: railway stall, college gate, market lane, office district

## Why This Can Win
- familiar fantasy, not imported fantasy
- simple enough for broad mobile audience
- strong progression loop
- culturally sticky and highly marketable in India

## Tagline Ideas
- Start Small. Brew Big.
- From One Kettle to Chai Empire.
- Build the busiest chai business in India.
