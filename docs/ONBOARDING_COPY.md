# Onboarding Copy

## Opening Splash
Start with one kettle. Build a chai legend.

## First-Time Intro
Your stall is ready.
Customers will arrive on their own.
Serve chai, earn coins, and grow step by step.

## First Goal Prompt
Buy an upgrade to brew chai faster.
More speed means more cups and more coins.

## Upgrade Tutorial
Upgrades help you serve faster, earn more, and keep customers happy.
Pick the boost your stall needs most.

## Menu Unlock Prompt
New items bring bigger orders.
Add crowd favorites to grow your business.

## Venue Progression Prompt
Your business is getting noticed.
Earn more to unlock your next shop.

## Offline Progress Message
Your chai shop stayed busy while you were away.
Collect your earnings and reopen the rush.

## Tone Notes
- warm, encouraging, and easy to read
- short lines for small screens
- simple words first, personality second
- celebrate progress without sounding loud or childish
