## Naming Rules
- Keep names short and easy to scan on mobile
- Use familiar anchor words like chai, tapri, adda, bazaar, kulhad
- Stay welcoming across India, avoid region-locked slang
- Let progression feel bigger, warmer, and more aspirational over time
