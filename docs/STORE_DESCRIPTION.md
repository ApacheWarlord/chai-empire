# Store Description

## Short Description
Build a humble chai stall into a bustling chai empire.

## App Store / Play Store Description
Build your own chai business in a cozy idle tycoon made for quick mobile sessions.

Start with a small roadside stall, serve hot chai automatically, earn coins, and upgrade every part of your setup. Brew faster, unlock tasty menu items, hire helpers, improve quality, and turn a simple tapri into a local favorite.

Grow through bigger venues, handle the office rush, enjoy offline earnings, and build a chai brand people love coming back to.

## Key Features
- Easy idle gameplay with satisfying upgrades
- Offline earnings while you are away
- Unlock chai varieties, snacks, and premium items
- Grow from tapri to cafe to full chai empire
- Warm India-inspired flavor with broad appeal
